Beef Stew

This beef stew recipe is cozy, hearty, and filling with tender beef, carrots, and potatoes simmered in a rich and flavorful red wine sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 2 pounds beef chuck cubes
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 pound potatoes
  • 3 celery sticks
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • 4 medium carrots sliced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prep ingredients.
  2. Coat beef cubes in flour shaking excess off.
  3. Add olive oil to medium heat brown cubes in two batches.
  4. Remove beef add onions and celery sautee for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in garlic and tomato paste, stirring often, for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Scrape off brown bits from pan. Stirring in wine, broth, Worcestershire sauce.
  7. Add beef back to pot with bay leaves. Bring the soup to a boil, cover, reduce heat, simmer at medium low heat while occasionally stirring.
  8. Stir in potatoes and carrots covering potfor an hour, or till nice and tender. Stirring occasionally.
  9. Take bay leaves out stir in frozen peas till warmed.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste and enjoy!
