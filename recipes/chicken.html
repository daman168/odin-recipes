Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 celery stalks, thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots, thinly slices
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 10-12 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 pounds skinless, bone-in chicken breasts
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 ounces wide egg noodles
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in dutch oven set over medium heat.
  2. To the heated oil add chopped onions, celery carrots; cook for 5 minutes stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in garlic cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add chicken broth, stri browned bits. Add thyme, bay leaf; increase heat to high and bring mixture to a simmer.
  5. Add chicken breasts, salt, pepper; stir, then, cover and simmer over medium heat for 25-30 minutes, or until chicken is done.
  6. Remove chicken breasts from the pot and transfer to cutting board.
  7. Add noodles to broth cook for 6-7 minutes, or until al dente.
  8. In the meantime, shred chicken and discard the bones. Stir shredded chicken into soup; cover and simmer for 2 minutes.
  9. Turn off heat. Discard bay leaves, taste for salt and adjust accordingly.
  10. Garnish with parsley enjoy with lemon wedges.
